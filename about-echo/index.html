About us

Echo Imaging was founded in 1997
by professionals from the burgeoning
branded data storage industry, which at
that time was dominated by floppy diskettes.

Over time we expanded into loading
proprietary client data onto optical media
to facilitate practical applications,
such as product manuals, employee training
and POS equipment software operating
primarily for retail distribution networks.

The next shift was to pair these technologies
with branded merchandise for our clients’
use at tradeshows and events.

Today, we are a full-service provider of
branded media and promotional items that
 help clients elevate their brand in any venue
they connect with their customers.

  • We establish in-house CD printing and duplication, along with packaging solutions
