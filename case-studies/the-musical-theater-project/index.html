Case Study 3 |  Client > The Musical Theater Project

An important mission of TMTP is to document the lives of legendary American musical theater artists. And we share their passion. Echo Imaging has helped them achieve their goal by creating audio CDs with beautiful story-telling packaging, honoring famous contributors such as Sheldon Harnick “Fiddler on the Roof”, John Kander “Cabaret”, Harvey Schmidt “Philemon”, and Maxine Sullivan “Great Songs of the Cotton Club” (Grammy Nominee).

  • Challenge
    How do you capture a brilliant artist’s legacy and do justice to one of their creations representing their life’s work?
  • Solution
    You work and engage personally with everyone involved- artist’s agents, graphic designers, custom printers, replication factories and maintain the highest standard of excellence to assure consistent compliance with the record label’s brand standards.

In 2016, Harbinger Records (a subsidiary of The Musical Theater Project), and label founder Ken Bloom, were awarded a Grammy Award. In his acceptance speech, Ken thanked Echo Imaging, and Barb Milloy specifically, for her usual professionalism and vigilance in shepherding the packaging for this project from conception through production.
