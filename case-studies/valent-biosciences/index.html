Case Study 2  |  Client > Valent BioSciences

A global leader in the development and production of biorational products used in agricultural productivity, protecting public health, and keeping our forests beautiful, they offer sustainable solutions that reduce negative environmental impact.

This successful partnership is the result of our company’s adaption of Valent BioSciences’ vision. The following highlights some of the successful results we’ve been able to achieve for them.

  • Challenge
    How do you create meaningful messaging to help retain talent and ease the transition for employees in a new company acquisition?
  • Solution
    After brainstorming with our client, we came up with the idea of creating personalized (names included) welcome kits, which included apparel, drinkware and tech items for all employees. These kits were personally delivered by Valent BioSciences leadership.

  • Challenge
    Creating an online store which offers a large selection of high-end branded merch, while catering to their public health audience.
  • Solution
    Actualizing their vision, we coordinated and digitized eight anatomically correct mosquito species, and prepared those designs for decoration with embroidery and screen-printing.

Over the years, Echo has provided Valent BioSciences with numerous solutions and services.
Customized high end products for both national and international distribution through in-house events and trade shows, employee onboarding, and corporate events.
